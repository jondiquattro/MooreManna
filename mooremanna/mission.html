Our mission was born out of an overflowing heart to serve others, when we began in September of 2025 we were serving 15 meals. As of January 2026 we serve over 100 meals each week to those in need across Moore County.

Our desire is to begin having serve days across our county in impoverished areas to promote restoration and impart hope. As we continue to grow our mission.
