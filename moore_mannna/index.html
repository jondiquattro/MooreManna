Our Mission

We feed, empower and equip those in need across Moore County through Christ-centered discipleship.

"...I therefore command you, ‘Open your hand to the poor and needy neighbor in your land'"
Deuteronomy 15:11

Our Commitment

In everything we do, we seek to bring glory to Jesus Christ. We serve with honesty, transparency, and the strength that God provides (1 Peter 4:11).

We believe that no person is beyond the reach of God’s grace. Our teams act as a "beacon of hope" in the darkness, trusting that God can rebuild even the most broken lives.

Feed.
We feed the hungry both in body and spirit by delivering a hot meal and sharing the Gospel of Jesus Christ
Empower.
We empower individuals to find hope, healing, and the strength to overcome their current struggle by way of Christ-centered discipleship
Equip.
We equip people with the practical tools, skills, and spiritual foundation necessary to sustain a transformed life
