About Us

Our Mission

We feed, empower and equip those in need across Moore County through Christ-centered discipleship.

"...I therefore command you, ‘Open your hand to the poor and needy neighbor in your land' " - Deuteronomy 15:11

Our Commitment

In everything we do, we seek to bring glory to Jesus Christ. We serve with honesty, transparency, and the strength that God provides. We believe that no person is beyond the reach of God’s grace. Our teams act as a "beacon of hope" in the darkness, trusting that God can rebuild even the most broken lives.

“Whoever speaks, as one who speaks oracles of God; whoever serves, as one who serves by the strength that God supplies—in order that in everything God may be glorified through Jesus Christ. To him belong glory and dominion forever and ever. Amen.” - 1 Peter 4:11

Our Initiatives

FEED

We do not just provide a meal; we believe in meeting urgent physical needs with the highest level of dignity and grace.

EMPOWER

We empower individuals by recognizing their God-given potential and helping them find hope, healing, and the strength to overcome their current struggle by way of Christ-centered discipleship.

EQUIP

We equip our neighbors with the practical tools, skills, and spiritual foundation necessary to sustain a transformed life.

“I was hungry and you gave me something to eat...” - Matthew 25:36
