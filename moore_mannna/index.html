About Us

Our Mission

Our Mission is to feed, empower and equip those in need across the county with biblical principles and support. In addition to delivering a hot meal each week, we aim to bridge the gap between resources in the county and those in need of said resources. We work alongside multiple churches and nonprofits to feed, clothe and love those in need.

“Behold, how good and how pleasant it is for brethren to dwell together in unity!” – Psalm 133:1

Our Commitment

Our ministry is committed to alleviating hunger both in body and spirit in the county. Join us in our mission through volunteering, donations, or spreading awareness.

“And let us consider one another to provoke unto love and to good works...” – Hebrews 10:24-25
