Who We Are

Feed Empower Equip is a faith-based nonprofit ministry dedicated to serving those in need throughout our county. We believe that meeting physical needs opens doors to spiritual hope.

Our Heart

We serve the homeless, struggling families, and individuals facing hardship. Our volunteers partner with churches and community organizations to bring meals, clothing, hygiene supplies, and encouragement.

“Freely ye have received, freely give.” – Matthew 10:8

Our Vision

We envision a united community where churches, organizations, and individuals work together to eradicate hunger and restore dignity — both physically and spiritually.
