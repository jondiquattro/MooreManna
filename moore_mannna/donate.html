Ways You Can Give

Financial Contributions

Monetary donations help us purchase food, hygiene supplies, and essential resources for weekly outreach efforts.

Food Donations

Non-perishable food items, bulk ingredients, and meal supplies help us continue providing hot meals each week.

Clothing & Hygiene Items

New or gently used clothing, blankets, socks, and hygiene kits restore dignity and comfort to those we serve.

Corporate & Church Sponsorships

Partner with us as a business or ministry to sponsor outreach events, meal programs, or supply drives.

Our Mission in Action

In addition to hot meals, we distribute essential supplies like heaters, blankets,

hygiene kits, and shelf-stable groceries when appropriate.


We facilitate access to grants and scholarships for drug and alcohol

rehabilitation centers as well as transportation when needed.


Our goal is to be helpful, share the Gospel, and bridge the gap for

local resources. This year we have plans for area clean-ups,

communal garden planting and education, as well as home repairs.


Your partnership makes this happen.

Please consider joining us through donations or volunteering.
